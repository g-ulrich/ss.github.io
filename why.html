The Data

Early in the summer of 2017 I was invited to preach in a rural church up in Nothern PA. As I prepared for my message on Matthew 28, Jesus' words struck my heart in utter shame... "go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them..."

I thought, "when have I ever baptized anyone, let alone make disciples?" As a young man of 22 years old I just realized that I am not truly a follower of Christ.
